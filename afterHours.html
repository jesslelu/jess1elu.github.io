Peer Support Center - Volunteer 2020-2021

My mom worked as a nurse at Queensway Hospital, so I grew up being taught the value of looking out for other people.

Ever since highschool, I've been determined to help promote mental health awareness, and I decided to translate passion to action through volunteering.

Queen's Peer Support Centre's mantra is: No problem is too big or too small, we care about them all

At PSC, anyone can tune in for a 'talk' session and PSC volunteers like myself listen, empathize and offer resources that might help.

Canadian Undergraduate Conference on AI - Design team member 2020

In QMIND - Queen's AI hub - my team and I developed an image classifier to identify whether a patient has diabetic retinopathy based on eye images.

Since I was new to machine learning, this was a great learning experience, and our team had the opportunity to present our work at the CUCAI conference in Toronto!

My role was to research and implement image preprocessing methods.
